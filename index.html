Potauth

The potato-based authentication system

How does it work?

Potauth uses only potatoes to identify you. To make this possible, the login process has three steps:

  • Step 1: You enter your username and your favourite kind of potato
  • Step 2: You send an image of a potato (preferably square), your favourite one!
  • Step 3: You have now officially entered the Potatoverse!

Now, when you want to log in to your account, you'll use your username and favourite potato type. You will have to verify that it's you by selecting your potato from a list of cropped potato images before you can log in, though!
