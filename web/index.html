Potauth

The potato-based authentication system

How does it work?

Potauth uses only potatoes to identify you. To make this possible, the login process has three steps:

  • Step 1: You enter your username and your favourite kind of potato
  • Step 2: You send an image of a potato (preferably square), your favourite one!
  • Step 3: You will be given a "potato code," which will act as your password

Now, when you want to log in to your account, you'll use your username, favourite potato type, and your potato code. You will have to verify that it's you by selecting your potato from a list of cropped potato images before you can log in, though!
